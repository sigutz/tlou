High alert

 Date:01.01.2021

The Last of Us will become a TV show

HBO greenlit the series on November 20. PlayStation Productions's Asad Qizilbash and Carter Swan were named executive producers, and Word Games a production company. In January 2021, the Mighty Mint joined production, and Kantemir Balagov was announced as the pilot episode's director.
